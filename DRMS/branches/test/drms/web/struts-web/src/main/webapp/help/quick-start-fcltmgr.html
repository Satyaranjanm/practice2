Your Responsibility

  • Add/Update service agreement information of the participant site.
  • Check program participation status of the service agreement occasionally.

Control DR Mode

  1. Log In with your user name and password.
  2. Click the "MySite" tab.
  3. Click the "Add New Service Agreement" button.
  4. Fill out the service agreement profile of the participant as follows.

    Service Agreement details
    Service Agreement ID 10-digit PG&E service agreement number of the new site. SAID is not changeable after the first entry.
    Service Agreement Name Create nickname for the Service Agreement. The nickname can be assigned whatever as long as it represents the SA, including building name (eg, Irvington High), street address (eg, 50 Douglas), city (Oracle Rocklin), etc. One SA may have single or multiple revenue meters, though they will be totalized for a SA on InterAct.
    Organization Enter name of the organization of the service agreement (e.g., Contra Costa, Oracle)
    Comm Dev Name You don't have to setup.

  5. Fill out program participation status of the service agreement as follows.

    Program Participation Status
    Preparation Steps Lead Actor Action
    STEP 1:
    CPP Agreement Executed.     		Account Manager Make the status of the Dropdown Menu to "Yes" when the account is signed up for CPP agreement. If the account is already CPP, make it "Yes".
    STEP 2:
    InterAct Setup     		Operator This is primarily done by the Operator. Make the status of the dropdown menu to "Yes" when the interval meter is installed. If the account already has interval meter running, just make it "Yes".

  6. Click the "Save" Button.

Check Status

  1. Log in with your username and password.
  2. Click on the "Summary" tab. Unless tab is already displayed.
  3. Find your Service Agreement Name to see status of the SA. Go to Tab Guide page for definition of each field items on "Summary" tab.
